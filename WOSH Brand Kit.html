07

Voice & tone

Lead with the number

DO — PLAIN, CONFIDENT

  • "Your washer is on the way."
  • "12 min · Marina Heights, Tw 2."
  • "Done. Looking sharp."
  • "Saved AED 35 vs single bookings."
  • "Reschedule up to 1 hour before."

DON'T — SALESY, EMOJI, EXCLAIM

  • "🚗 Hey! Booking your awesome wash!!"
  • "Get sparkling clean ASAP! ✨"
  • "Woohoo, all done! 🎉🎉"
  • "Our amazing team is on the way!"
  • "Game-changing convenience awaits."

RULES THAT MATTER MOST

USE

  • One signature accent — --wosh-accent
  • Space Grotesk for everything, Plex Mono for labels
  • 2-digit mono section indices (01 / SELECT WASH)
  • Hero numerals at 120 / 0.9, right-aligned
  • Dotted dividers between groups (receipt feel)
  • 4px grid · 2px brutalist radii · pill CTAs

AVOID

  • A second accent color
  • Inter, Roboto, system-stack body fonts
  • Decorative SVG illustrations
  • Emoji in product UI
  • Drop shadows on cards
  • Replacing hero numerals with icons
